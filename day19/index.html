How To Play

Guess the Wordle in 6 tries.

  • Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word.
  • The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.
  • Green tile shows that letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  • Orange tile shows that letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  • Grey tile shows that letter is not in the word
